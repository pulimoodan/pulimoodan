About me.

Hello! My name is Akbar Ali and I enjoy creating things that live on the internet. My interest in web development started back in 2017 when I was studying in 8th grade. I was curious about how software and web applications work - turns out an android app taught me a lot about HTML & CSS!

Fast-forward to today, and I've had the privilege of working as a freelance web developer and making numerous web applications for my clients. My main focus these days is building accessible, inclusive products and digital experiences for a variety of clients.

I also made some personal projects and contributed to the open-source community as I found it more enjoyable to connect with experienced developers.

I'm a web developer with a focus on the MERN stack and Django, but still exploring other technologies and frameworks that catch my interest! if you're looking for a developer to add to your team, I'd love to hear from you!

Claystones

Claystones is a venture dedicated to providing authentic handmade pottery designs in various forms worldwide. The client provided the designs and requested to develop a landing page with slides and carousels. The design got slightly modified during the development process according to the client's content. Simple minimalistic design with 3 combinations of colors. The page starts with a simple navbar including Claystones logo. Following with a carousel and an image in the background.

The project was completed in almost a week and the client asked for some modifications. They told me to remove the footer links because there were no more pages right then.

Publiso

Publiso was one of my personal projects which was later published as an open-source. This is an online platform to read ebooks of famous authors. I implemented all features for almost 2 weeks. The readers have to make an account to read the ebooks. They can surf through the platform and filter books according to read count and authors.

They can also view the full list of authors and their profiles. There is a feature to add books to your library and also write reviews about the books you read. I made an admin panel with Django's built-in admin system.
